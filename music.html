Concrete and Gold was released on September 15th, 2017 and is the ninth studio album by Foo Fighters

Sonic Highways is the 8th studio album released by the Foo Fighters. It produced hit songs such as Congregation and Something From Nothing

Released in 2011, Wasting Light, the 7th studio album, reached Number 1 in both the US and UK album charts.

The Foo Fighters Greatest Hits was the first compilation album released by the band in 2009 and has since gone platinum multiple times.

Echoes, Silence, Patience & Grace was released in 2007, topping the UK charts and hitting no.3 on the US.

In Your Honor is the fifth studio album released by the Foo Fighters and is their second highest selling album in the US.
